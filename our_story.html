Our Story

ADWALAH came as a calling but also represents friendship. The concept debuted in 2011 with the 16 Dresses Fashion and Art Exhibition shows in Accra and Cape Coast. The label ADWALAH, born in 2015, honors the names of friends and family, primarily that of two friends who bought us our first sewing machine. ADWALAH is deeply rooted in the idea of friendship because friends created the agency to start, it was in the company of friends that the vision to fearlessly continue the pursuit of this childhood dream was revealed. Friends were the first models, the first patrons and continue to be our biggest supporters.

The #ADbabe

The #ADbabe is a woman with an eye for unique, elegant pieces. She has a sharp mind, a sense of humor and is confident with her style. Our goal is to create pieces that celebrate this woman at every stage of her life - from casual Saturdays to birthdays, to graduations.

Our Values

For our bespoke creations, we enjoy the creative task of merging a woman’s vision of herself with our aesthetic, to create something that is truly authentic to each individual. To celebrate our #ADBabe, each item from a ready-to-wear collection is named after the woman who first wears it or inspired the piece’s creation. We believe the only fashion rule worth following is one’s (own) feelings, therefore we encourage women to invest solely in clothing that expresses this!

Meet the Team

With a college, friendship and an interest in women’s empowerment as commonalities between all 3 women, the ADWALAH team is united by a single vision that is driven by a diversity of personalities. Regine is the Founder and Creative Director. Inventive and spirited, she leads the team in the design and production of clothing and manages the overall direction of the brand. Maame is the Business Development Manager with a superior ability to manage processes from start to finish and on budget. Audrey, our Branding Manager is a Chemist with killer communication skills who spurs the #ADbabe on to continue living her fearless fabulous life. Buying a piece or sharing our story welcomes you into our fold of friendship and we’ll be sure to cheer you on through life!
